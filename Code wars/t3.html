Škoda RegioPanter Electric Trains: In September 2024, Bulgaria signed a €500 million deal with Škoda for 20 RegioPanter electric multiple units (EMUs). These trains, equipped with air conditioning and low-noise technology, will operate at a maximum speed of 160 km/h and accommodate over 300 passengers. This fleet aims to enhance the passenger experience with modern amenities and faster, eco-friendly travel across Bulgaria
