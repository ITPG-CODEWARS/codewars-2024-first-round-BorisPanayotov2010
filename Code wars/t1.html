Stadler Double-Decker Trains: Bulgaria signed a contract in April 2024 with Stadler Polska for seven double-decker trains, valued at approximately €153 million. These new trains are expected to bring enhanced capacity and comfort, especially for intercity travel, and will be delivered by 2026. The focus is on accessibility and efficiency, making them ideal for Bulgaria’s high-demand routes
